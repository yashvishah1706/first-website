Home-made ice cream is surprisingly easy, and tastes totally delicious with nuts added

  • Posted By : Rubel Ahmed
  • |
  • Time : 11.30 am

Ingredients

2 tbsp coffee powder
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup ful fat milk
1 teaspoon cornflour
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp freash cream

Combine instant coffee powderwith cornflour and 1/2 cup of coldmilkin a bowl mix well and keeep aside

Add coffee walnut mixture in semi set,remove from the freezer and blend in a misture until it is smooth
Once the mixture is semi-set place it in the freezer and serve it.
